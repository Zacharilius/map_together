MapTogether

Create Map then Share

A site for mapping together with friends, colleagues, and anyone with an internet connection.

Get started by creating an account and then creating or joining a map room. Your map state will automatically be saved as you zoom, pan, or create points, lines, and polygons. Only you can make changes to your map, however, anyone you share your map room link with can visit your map room and watch you create or work on your map.

This site is in active development. Message me on github for feature requests.
